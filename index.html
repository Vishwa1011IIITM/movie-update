The Best Movies according to Vishwa1011 :

My top five Movies of all time are :

  • Your Name

    - This is my favourite Anime movie. I love how well it is animated.

    Plot :
    Two teenagers share a profound, magical connection upon discovering they are swapping bodies.
    Things manage to become even more complicated when the boy and girl decide to meet in person.


    IMDb : 8.4/10

  • La La Land

    - This is one of the most beautiful romantic movies i have ever seen , with great cinematography.

    Plot :
    When Sebastian, a pianist, and Mia, an actress, follow their passion and achieve success in their respective fields, they find themselves torn between their love for each other and their careers.


    IMDb : 8/10

  • The Call

    - This is a very exciting thriller Korean movie from Netflix i watched recently.

    Plot :
    Connected by phone in the same home but 20 years apart, a serial killer puts another woman's past -- and life -- on the line to change her own fate.


    IMDb : 7.1/10

  • Interstellar

    This is directed by one of the best directors in the history of cinema, Christopher Nolan.
    This movie takes you on a sci-fi experience you have never had before, with an amazing plot.
    This is definetly a watch, 10/10!

    Plot :
    When Earth becomes uninhabitable in the future, a farmer and ex-NASA pilot, Joseph Cooper, is tasked to pilot a spacecraft, along with a team of researchers, to find a new planet for humans.


    IMDb: 8.7/10

  • Fight Club

    Directed by David Fincher is the marvelous movie that takes you on a suspense,thriller adventure where you will be lost till the first half finishes.
    This movie demands patience and delivers well for that time. A good watch, 8/10!

    Plot :
    Unhappy with his capitalistic lifestyle, a white-collared insomniac forms an underground fight club with Tyler, a careless soap salesman. Soon, their venture spirals down into something sinister.


    IMDb : 8.8/10
